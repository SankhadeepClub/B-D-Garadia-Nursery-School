About

Since its inception in 2015, B D Garodiya Nursery School has earned the trust and appreciation of parents for providing a strong foundation in early childhood education.

We follow a modern and child-friendly preschool curriculum that focuses on the overall development of every child. Our school offers a safe, engaging, and nurturing environment supported by well-designed infrastructure and experienced teachers. With age-appropriate teaching methods, we ensure that learning is fun, meaningful, and effective. Our smooth and transparent admission process makes it easy for parents to begin their child’s educational journey with confidence.

We are not just a daycare — we are committed to shaping young minds and building a bright future through quality preschool education.
